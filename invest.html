The Problem

400M+ Unbanked in Latin America

Traditional banking fails communities across the region. People rely on informal systems — but these have no transparency, no protection, and no digital infrastructure.

Informal ROSCAs

500B+ USD moves through informal rotating savings groups (tandas/cundinas) yearly. No records, no accountability, no recourse.

Banking Exclusion

70%+ of Hondurans lack access to credit. Traditional banks require collateral, formal employment, and minimum balances most can't meet.

Zero Transparency

No way to verify payments, track contributions, or resolve disputes. Trust is the only collateral — and it breaks down at scale.

Business Model

Multiple Revenue Streams

Platform fees across every financial touchpoint, scaling with user adoption.

Tanda Commissions

1-3% fee on each contribution cycle. With 100 active groups averaging $500/cycle, that's $1.5K-4.5K/month recurring.

Marketplace Fees

5-10% on product sales and service bookings. Seller subscriptions for premium tools and analytics.

Chain Transaction Fees

Every on-chain transaction pays gas in LTD. As platform activity migrates to chain, fee revenue grows.
